Why Choose Fix and Fit?

Fix and Fit is an indigenous organisation with international counterparts, totally engaged in the design, creation, development, manufacturing, and marketing of orthopaedics, prosthetics, orthotics and rehabilitation healthcare products. We work with top-tier branches and import advanced products and equipment from Germany, USA, UK, France, Japan, and Italy.

  • Certified Professionals: 15+ years of clinical experience in prosthetics, orthotics, and pedorthotics
  • International Standards: FDA-approved products meeting global quality benchmarks
  • Personalized Care: Custom solutions tailored to each patient's unique anatomy and needs
  • 24/7 Support: Comprehensive follow-up and emergency support services
  • Proven Results: 95% patient satisfaction rate with measurable improvements
