Our Mission

Fix and Fit Prosthetics and Orthotics is a company of highly trained and certified professionals dedicated to providing high-quality, personalized prosthetic, orthotic, and podorthotic solutions. We are indigenous to Africa and work with international partners, importing products from countries like Germany, the USA, the UK, France, and Japan.

Our mission is to empower patients by providing personalized, high-quality solutions. Our vision is to become a leading, sustainable company in Africa that provides complete prosthetic and orthotic services by competent professionals. Our motto is "Making you fit again".
